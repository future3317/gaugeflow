# D0.7 phase-1 rationale

D0.6 learned adjacent maps but failed direct long maps and repeated composition. Flow Map Matching frames two-time maps and their composition as the primary object; Consistency Models motivate agreement across temporal resolutions; improved consistency training motivates explicit long-horizon and self-consistency diagnostics. D0.7 therefore uses equal span strata, endpoint-map mass, an un-detached two-step rollout target, and a semigroup loss.
